Adventure Works Sales & Business Analysis Project

This project focuses on analyzing the Adventure Works dataset to extract meaningful business insights and support data-driven decision-making. The analysis covers key areas including sales performance, customer behavior, inventory management, and operational efficiency.

Sales performance analysis was conducted to evaluate revenue, profit, and order trends over time, along with identifying top-performing products, categories, and regions. Customer behavior insights were derived by segmenting customers based on purchasing patterns, identifying high-value customers, and analyzing retention and lifetime value.

The project also includes inventory and product analysis to identify best-selling and slow-moving products, optimize stock levels, and evaluate product-wise profitability. Additionally, operational efficiency was assessed by analyzing production costs, supply chain flow, and opportunities to reduce overall cost of sales.

Tools such as Excel, SQL, and Power BI were used for data cleaning, transformation, analysis, and dashboard creation. The final outcome provides actionable insights to improve business performance, enhance customer targeting, and optimize operations.
